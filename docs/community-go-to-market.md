# Community Go-To-Market Playbook

## Channels

- Reddit communities focused on digital wellbeing and habit change.
- Recovery-oriented creator partnerships.
- Product Hunt and browser extension directories.
- SEO content targeting focus and blocker comparisons.

## Launch sequence

1. Soft launch to waitlist and community testers.
2. Collect false positives and UX issues for two weeks.
3. Publish Chrome listing + updated site pricing.
4. Start content cadence: weekly educational resources.

## Messaging constraints

- Do not claim medical treatment or guaranteed cure outcomes.
- Be explicit about platform limits and consent requirements.
- Highlight practical outcomes: fewer impulsive visits, more focus time.

## KPI baseline

- Extension installs.
- 7-day activation rate.
- 30-day retention.
- False positive report rate.
- Free-to-Pro conversion.
