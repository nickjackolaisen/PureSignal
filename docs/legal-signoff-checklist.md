# Legal Signoff Checklist

Complete this before store submission.

- [ ] Terms language approved (`legal/terms-of-service.md`).
- [ ] Privacy language approved (`legal/privacy-policy.md`).
- [ ] Cookie language approved (`legal/cookie-policy.md`).
- [ ] Jurisdiction and refund policy approved.
- [ ] Data subject rights language approved.
- [ ] Accountability and platform-limitation claims approved.
- [ ] Final support email/URL approved.
- [ ] Counsel signoff recorded (or explicit draft waiver recorded).
