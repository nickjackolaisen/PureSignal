# PureSignal Voice and Tone

## Core principles

- Supportive and direct.
- Calm and practical.
- Non-shaming and non-judgmental.
- Focused on habits, focus, and accountability.

## Required wording guardrails

- Use language like "support", "focus", "digital wellbeing", and "accountability".
- Explain friction features clearly (for example, cooldowns) and why they exist.
- Use plain language when features fail ("We could not sync the blocklist. Try again in a minute.").

## Prohibited claims

- Do not claim to cure addiction.
- Do not claim guaranteed prevention of uninstall or disable actions.
- Do not imply guaranteed medical outcomes.

## UI copy style

- Keep labels short and clear.
- Keep destructive actions explicit ("Request disable for 30 minutes").
- Include consent reminders when partner notifications are enabled.
